Our Services

Our metal fabrication services don't end at gates and railings! We can produce structural and decorative ironwork to fit any requirements - including balconies and balustrades, safety rails, shutters as well as repair services.

RAILINGS

Our team can create bespoke railings for your access ramp and staircase. We can design, fabricate and install any type of handrail to suit your needs.

SHUTTERS

We manufacture and install high quality shutters for shop fronts and garages with a number of designs. If you are unsure, our team can advise you what would suit your requirements

REPAIRS AND SERVICING

We can cover all Greater London areas for any repairs or servicing works related to gates, railing, balconies or shutters. . Contact us for further information.

GATES (ELECTRIC AND MANUAL)

Gates can improve the security of your property. Electric gates allow you to gain access with electronic keys or a code entry system. We offer manual and automatic gate installation for both domestic and commercial properties. Your gates can be fitted with variety of automated access system that includes video entry.

BALCONIES

If you are looking to expand your living area with a balcony can help. Although balconies are not mentioned in the name…remember “if it’s metal we make it!” We provide a wide choice in open balconies. We design, manufacture and install balconies giving your living area a stunning view. We also manufacture and install custom made Juliet Balconies as well.
